## What is this tool? 

It’s a smart, frictionless group expense splitter designed to handle complex bills without the headache of manual itemization. Instead of forcing you to log every single line item on a receipt, it operates on an "Exception-Based" model. You just input the total bill and tag the outliers (the things not everyone shared), and the engine dynamically calculates the exact mathematical breakdown for the whole group.

## The 3-Step User Flow

* **Step 1:** The Roster: Define the party size. Add specific names if necessary and quickly toggle their subgroup tags (e.g., Drinker/Non-Drinker).  
    
* **Step 2:** The Exceptions: Input the total grand bill. Add specific items that weren't shared equally, and use the quick-assign buttons to allocate those costs to the right individuals or subgroups.  
    
* **Step 3:** The Settlement: Review the dynamically generated math. Select who paid the bill, and hit export to drop the final settlement instructions into your group chat.

## Core Features

* Exception-Based Math (Quick Layer): Simply enter the grand total and list only the exceptions (e.g., "Extra beers" or "Special dessert"). The system automatically subtracts the exceptions, splits the base amount equally among the group, and accurately adds the exception costs back to the specific consumers.  
    
* Detailed Split Fallback: For completely chaotic bills where nothing is shared, you can leave the grand total blank and itemize everything line-by-line (with an optional AI-powered receipt scanner for automatic extraction).  
    
* Rapid Party Setup: Skip typing out a dozen names. Punch in your total party size (e.g., "8"), and the system auto-generates the slots. You only need to type the names of people attached to exceptions.  
    
* Smart Subgroup Tagging: Users are assigned quick tags (currently Veg/Non-Veg and Drinks/No Drinks). This allows you to assign a shared exception (like a pitcher of drinks) to a specific subgroup with a single tap, rather than selecting names one by one.  
    
* Automated Settlement Engine: Select the person who fronted the cash for the bill, and the app instantly generates exact peer-to-peer payout instructions (e.g., "Person A owes Person B ₹450").  
    
* Frictionless Export: One-click buttons to either copy a clean, formatted summary straight to WhatsApp, or download a visually polished UI card as an image.
