# How It Works: The Ultimate Bill Splitter

No more awkward bill splitting.

Most dinners aren’t equal. Some people drink, some don’t, some order extra.
This tool handles that without making you enter everything manually.

---

## Flow 1: The Quick Split (For Fast & Easy Outings)
*Best for: Simple meals, drinks with friends, or when most items are shared equally.*

1. **Set the Scene:** Simply enter the total number of people involved. There's no need to painstakingly log every individual item on the receipt.
2. **Enter the Total:** Input the final bill amount.
3. **Log the Exceptions:** Instead of listing what everyone *did* have, only add the items that *weren't* shared by the whole group (e.g., someone's extra cocktail, a specific appetizer). Assign these exceptions to the specific people who ordered them.
4. **Instant Calculation:** The app does the math instantly. It evenly divides the base shared amount among everyone and accurately adds the exception costs only to the relevant individuals, giving you a precise split in seconds.

---

## Flow 2: The Detailed Split (For Complex Group Dinners)
*Best for: Large groups, diverse dietary preferences, and complex receipts with tips and taxes.*

1. **Add People & Preferences:** Enter the names of everyone in the group. You can tag their specific preferences upfront (e.g., Veg, Non-Veg, Drinks, Zen/No Drinks) to make assigning items later incredibly fast.
2. **Scan the Receipt:** Take a picture or upload an image of the bill. The built-in AI automatically reads the receipt and extracts all the items, quantities, and prices.
3. **Verify with Toggles:** Quickly review the extracted list. Use simple, intuitive toggles to verify who ate or drank what, leveraging the preference tags you set earlier.
4. **Handle Taxes & Tips:** Enter the final amount paid (the total bill) or add GST, service charges, and tips individually. The app will proportionally distribute these extra charges across everyone's individual share.
5. **Record the Payer:** Specify who actually paid the bill so the app knows who should be reimbursed.
6. **Get the Split:** Instantly receive a perfectly accurate breakdown of who owes what to whom. You can download the final summary as an image or copy the text directly to WhatsApp to share with the group.

---

## Core Steps at a Glance

1. **Add People:** Enter names or just a headcount. Tag preferences (Veg/Non-Veg, Drinks/No Drinks) if desired.
2. **Add Bill & Exceptions:** Input the total amount manually or upload a receipt for automatic AI extraction. Pinpoint the items that weren't shared by everyone.
3. **Get the Split:** The app handles the heavy lifting—splitting the shared amount, factoring in exception costs, and detailing exactly who owes whom. Copy, share to WhatsApp, or download as an image!
